VIDEO 02

BLØF = Anime Music (read description)

The 2022 opening for 'One Piece' features the song 'PAINT' by 'I Don't Like Mondays'. When I heard this song I Immediately realized it sounded suspiciously similar to 'Alles is liefde' (2007) by Dutch poprock band 'BLØF'. I isolated the instrumental of 'PAINT' and the vocals of 'Alles is liefde' to create this mash-up. The joke here being that this mainstream Dutch pop hit from 2007 shares surprisingly many similarities with the music commonly associated with animated shows produced in Japan. I made the video to further sell the idea that BLØF = anime music.

VIDEO 03

Short form content

Zsofia Marx is a sculptural artist using felt who makes her living from selling workshops. Most of the participants are part of the niche subculture of felting, and Zsofia is beloved within this community. I filmed and edited this video with the explicit purpose of reaching a wider audience, allowing Zsofia to sell workshops to more beginners/newcomers. I tried to capture the stimulating nature of videos showcasing the process of some form of handcraft, and let the instagram algorithm do the rest. This was my first attempt at something like this, and it was incredibly successful with 13k likes and 422 comments in the first 48 hours.
